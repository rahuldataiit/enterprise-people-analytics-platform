# Project Charter

Project Name: Enterprise People Analytics Platform

Purpose:
- Provide an end-to-end people analytics platform using a SQL Data Warehouse, medallion architecture, and Power BI to support enterprise decision making.

Scope:
- Ingest and transform HR and business data, create a curated analytics layer, and deliver dashboards and KPI reports.

Key Stakeholders:
- HR Leadership
- Data Engineering
- Business Analysts
- IT/Security

Objectives:
- Establish a single source of truth for people metrics.
- Implement data lineage and governance.
- Deliver actionable dashboards.

Success Criteria:
- Reliable daily/near-real-time ETL pipelines.
- Documented data dictionary and business rules.
- Dashboards in Power BI adopted by stakeholders.
