# KPI Catalogue

Catalogue of Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) delivered by the platform.

Example KPIs:
- Headcount: Current active headcount, by department and location.
- Attrition Rate: (Separations over period) / (Average headcount during period)
- Time to Fill: Average days to fill open positions.

For each KPI include:
- Definition, formula, data sources, owner, update frequency, and visualization notes.
